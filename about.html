About Hostel Harmony

Hostel Harmony is a modern complaint management system designed to streamline the process of reporting and resolving issues in hostels. Our platform empowers students to report issues related to electricity, water, cleanliness, WiFi, food, and more, with the ability to attach images or videos for proof and tag their location (room, block, hostel).

With features like an issue tracking dashboard, a community forum for peer help, an admin panel for hostel authorities, and a feedback system, Hostel Harmony ensures efficient communication between students and hostel management. Our goal is to create a harmonious living environment for all hostel residents.

Built with a futuristic UI, Hostel Harmony combines functionality with aesthetics, featuring a gradient background, glassmorphism effects, and constellation particles for an engaging user experience.
